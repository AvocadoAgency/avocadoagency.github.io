MICHAEL CHENG is a Digital Matte Painter and Concept Artist, his work has appeared in TV shows, commercials, and a soon to be released feature film.

SKILLS
Digital Matte Painting: complete environments, set extension, set enhancement, set replacement, virtual sets and locations, and 2.5D camera projection. Compositing: combining live action, 3D, green screen, camera tracking, paint and rotoscoping. 3D: modeling, texturing, shading, lighting and rendering. Concepts. Storyboarding. Illustration.

TOOLS
Adobe Photoshop, The Foundry NUKE X, The Foundry Modo, Autodesk Maya, Autodesk Mudbox, and V Ray/Mental Ray.

EDUCATION
B.F.A Illustration 2015, Art Center College of Design, Emphasis in Entertainment Arts

WHERE AM I LOCATED?
Los Angeles, California.
